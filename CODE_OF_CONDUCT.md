# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Provide helpful feedback and collaborate politely

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination
- Personal attacks, threats, or doxxing
- Sharing private information without consent

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers:
- Email: **neuronest-help@helper.com** 

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content, close issues/PRs, or restrict participation for violations.
